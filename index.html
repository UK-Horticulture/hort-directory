This interactive map shows the locations of resources related to Horticultural Activity across the Commonwealth of Kentucky.


Clicking/tapping an icon on the map shows shows detail.


The +/- buttons at the top left zoom in and out, as does the mouse wheel, and standard mobile gestures.


Map resources can be turned on and off by type in the legend at the top right.


The search box allows users to enter a physical address, city name, or zipcode to place a marker.


The primary data source is the Governor's Office of Agricultural Policy. Funding and support provided by the Kentucky Agricultural Development Board through the Kentucky Horticulture Council.
